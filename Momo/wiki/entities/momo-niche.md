# MOMO niche

The channel niche is **faceless "ranking" Shorts — Top-N list videos.** (Dropped the earlier
"Peter & Stewie" / character-dialogue framing, 2026-06-23 — that format still exists in the
tooling as an option but is not the channel's identity.)

Goal of every video: **best-effort, aimed at going viral** — strong hook in the first seconds,
tight pacing, clear payoff.
